FAQ

There are some HatSploit frequently asked questions

1. Why HatSploit instead of other InfoSec frameworks?

We have implemented a system of modules, payloads and plugins that is understandable to every beginner. Thus, everyone can fill HatSploit with new modules, payloads or plugins.

2. What about new RCE, LFI and other PoCs?

We monitor in real time the emergence of vulnerabilities and PoC scripts for their exploitation and immediately publish them in the new release of HatSploit Framework.

3. What happens after successful RCE module execution?

After RCE vulnerability has been exploited, HatSploit sends and executes the specified payload. The quality of exploitation is the most important for us.

4. How many people working on this project?

I am working on this and other projects alone, although I declare that this is a team, but I am alone there.
